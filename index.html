FLOOD ALERT SYSTEM
IMFORMATION

A flood alert system is essential for protecting lives and property from flooding. It uses sensors and satellite technology to monitor rainfall and water levels in real-time. Data analysis predicts potential flooding through hydrological models and historical data. When risks are identified, alerts are sent via SMS, emails, and social media, ensuring timely communication with the public. Community education on flood safety and preparedness is vital, and after events, the system is reviewed to improve future responses.
